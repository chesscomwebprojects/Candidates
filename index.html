About the Tour

The Champions Chess Tour comprises four events and one live in-person Final. With the very best players in the world and a $1.7 million prize fund, this is Chess.com's most important event of the year.

Learn more

The event features a $1,700,000 prize fund, with $300,000 for each online event and $500,000 for the live Finals.

Players compete in the four online events for a spot in the live Finals and CCT tour points. The player who wins the CCT Finals is the tour's champion.
